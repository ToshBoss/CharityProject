CharityMiner?
What even is this site?
Well, this site uses a Javascript code to mine Verge Coins using your CPU (Central Processing Unit).
Where does this money go?
This money, after you and others have mined enough coins, will be converted into either USD or Euros and then 91% goes to Charity.
Where does the 9% go?
The 9% of money that is earned goes to the contributor of this website (Find us here).
What happens when I sign up?
The website creates an account for you (You have to provide email address, and name), and then it keeps track of how many Verge coins you have mined.
What can I do with the progress I have made?
You have bragging rights to all the people you met and you can tell them how much of a good person you are by showing them your progress in mining for Charity.
