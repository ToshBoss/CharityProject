Will this affect my computer?

Depending on the speed that you set the miner to, the CPU can experience a higher level of workload, but OSX and Windows have built in protections to "throttle" the CPU to keep it in safe tempratures and loads. You may want to set the speed lower if you want to keep the computer cool and running at a regular pace.
